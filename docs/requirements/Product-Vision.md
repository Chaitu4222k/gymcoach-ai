# Product Vision

## Project Name

GymCoach AI

---

## Vision Statement

GymCoach AI is an AI-powered personal fitness coach that helps users achieve their fitness goals through personalized workout planning, workout tracking, progress analysis, and intelligent recommendations.

---

## Target Users

People who regularly go to the gym and want guidance to improve their fitness journey.

---

## Supported Goals

- Fat Loss
- Muscle Gain
- Strength
- Endurance
- General Fitness
- Calisthenics

---

## Core Philosophy

The application should function like a personal coach rather than a workout generator.

It should continuously analyze the user's profile, workouts, recovery, and goals to provide meaningful recommendations.

---

## Success Criteria

A user should be able to:

- Create a profile
- Receive a personalized workout plan
- Track workouts
- Monitor progress
- Receive AI-powered recommendations
- Improve consistency over time

---

## Out of Scope (Version 1)

- Payments
- Gym memberships
- Trainer marketplace
- Social networking
- Wearable integrations
- Video streaming
- Mobile application
